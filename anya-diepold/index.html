Hi, I'm Anya.

I was born in the Bavarian Alps in Germany, close to the border to Austria in October 1989. Since I can remember I loved drawing and crafting and here on this webpage you'll find some of my artwork, process and information about commission work.

I'm a self-taught artist and specialise in contemporary, surreal oil portraits and pet portraits in pastel. Furthermore I'm currently working on adding graphic design, illustrations and digital painting to my skill set.

When I'm not painting or drawing, I read, crochet or cross stitch. During longer breaks, I like to travel and explore new countries, cultures and cuisines.
